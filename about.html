IPLiN

Intellectual Property Licensing Network

IPLiN is a platform that is short for Intellectual Property Licensing Network

IPLiN was started by FSU students Riley Garrison, Matthew Klopfenstein, and Alexy Ostrovidov

What we hope to accomplish with IPLiN is to provide a platform for users to view Intellectual Property and Licenses.

Currently, IPLiN allows users to search, add to, delete from, and update records the database.
